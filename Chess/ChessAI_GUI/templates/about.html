ChessAI - About

Our game offers an advanced chess experience with two modes: playing against a bot or playing online.

About the Game

ChessAI is a smart chess game that utilizes artificial intelligence and physical boards to allow you to play against a bot or other players by connecting their board to the website. This way, the pieces moved physically will also move on the screen! Our goal is to provide an enjoyable and challenging chess experience with advanced tools.

Our Features

  • Play against a bot with different difficulty levels
  • Option to play online against other players by connecting a physical board to the website
  • Simple and user-friendly experience
  • Mobile-friendly and accessible across all devices
